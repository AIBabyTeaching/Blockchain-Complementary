What exactly is an Ethereum account?

Read this first

An Ethereum account is an identity on the blockchain. It has an address, a balance, and a nonce.

A wallet is the tool that holds the private key. A signer is the part that actually proves ownership when sending a transaction.

In this lab, you will send native ETH between local Hardhat accounts and observe three things immediately:

  • the sender balance decreases
  • the receiver balance increases
  • the sender nonce increments by one

This is the simplest honest blockchain transaction you can show before adding smart-contract logic.
