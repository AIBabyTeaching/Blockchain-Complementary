Why voting is the right next step

Transition from Lab 2

A vote is still a transaction, but unlike Lab 2 it does not move ETH between two accounts. Instead, it calls a smart contract that applies rules to shared on-chain state.

The contract decides whether the action is allowed. In this lab, that means:

  • only deployed proposals can be chosen
  • each address can vote once
  • voting closes automatically at the deadline

That is the key move from “transactions exist” to “contract logic governs what a transaction is allowed to do.”
